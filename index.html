Our Donation Plan

We have plaaned to give donation in a unique way. We want to assist some family with food, shelter and education

Food for a family

Food is a basic necessity for survival and also the key to a healthy life for any lives. Without regular healthy meals children struggle to go to school, carers struggle to work and communities are left battling to survive.

Shelter for a family

After food, the second basic necessity for survival is shelter. So everyone should be ensured to live in a secured shelter. So, if you guys support us by donating, we will try our best.

Education for a child

By ensuring a child's education, we can decrease poverty also. Education is also a basic need. So we can ensure education for child, if you all support us to help those people.
